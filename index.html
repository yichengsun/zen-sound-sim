tending the garden

Three ceramic spirits live here. They answer touch with sound — slowly, the way a garden answers care.

tap
click — a ceramic tone, a knock, a pluck
repeated tapping
rapid clicks — seeds a quiet rhythm that slowly fades
press
click and hold — a drone swells; the longer you stay, the warmer it grows
hug
keep holding (~3s) — a rare warm bloom that deepens the whole garden
caress
drag slowly — breath and shimmer follow your hand
faces & ears
the intimate zones — small voice-like answers; ears open the stereo field
visitors
invite virtual visitors (top left) — pick a personality, or toggle "let the garden choose" to have it invite and release visitors on its own, continuously, until you turn it off. up to six can be present at once. they tend the garden gently and make way when you're active. holding several sculptures at once, together, is rare and worth staying for.

Gentle, sustained touch is rewarded most: as the garden warms, chords and small melodies begin to grow underneath everything. Left alone, the spirits murmur in their sleep.
